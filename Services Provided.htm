Services Provided

Our mission is to support individuals with I/DD from their first breath to the last so that they are able to live their lives fully engaged in their communities. We provide a wide variety of services to people with I/DD and their families including but not limited to individual and public policy advocacy; residential, educational, and vocational services; person-centered and financial planning; recreational activities; and other supports that meet the unique needs of the community.
